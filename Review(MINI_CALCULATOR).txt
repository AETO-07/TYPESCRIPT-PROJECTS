The Mini Calculator implements core arithmetic operations and input validation in a compact UI.

What it does:
* Accepts two numeric inputs and performs add, subtract, multiply, divide operations.
* Validates inputs and prevents division by zero.
* Provides a clear/reset button to reset inputs and results.

Notes & lessons learned:
* Straightforward DOM wiring and separation of small helper functions (`getInput`, `validateInputs`).
* Good error messages for incomplete input and divide-by-zero.

Potential improvements:
* Support decimal numbers and keyboard input for a smoother UX.
* Improve formatting of results and handle edge cases like very large numbers.
* Consider combining into a single calculator that supports expression parsing.

Overall: Practical utility with clear separation of concerns and easy to extend.
